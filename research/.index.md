# research — homepage marketing research (not deployed)

Supporting material behind the bldgtyp.com homepage rewrite. Everything derives
from the "Marketing Passivhaus" podcast (marketingpassivhaus.com, host James
Turner) — 38 episodes scraped, noted, and synthesized into positioning rules and
homepage copy. Not part of deploy output (`site/` only). See `../.index.md`.

## Contents

### `transcripts/`
38 full episode transcripts (one `.md` per guest, slug = guest name; plus `trailer.md`). Scraped verbatim with timestamps. Source URL in each file header.

### `episode-notes/`
38 per-episode summaries paralleling `transcripts/` (same slugs). Each has sections: Summary, Key Marketing Takeaways, Language & Framing. Links back to its transcript.

### `synthesis/` — the distilled outputs
- `marketing-rules.md` — master rules distilled from all 38 episodes, each cited to `[Guest — slug]`, HIGH-CONSENSUS flags. The upstream source-of-truth for the other synthesis docs. `marketing-rules.html` is a rendered copy.
- `homepage-alignment-and-business-model.md` — audits `site/index.html` against the rules (messaging + business-model lenses).
- `homepage-copy-redline.md` — proposed homepage copy edits; carries a STATUS block tracking what's implemented vs. staged in the working tree.
- `positioning-decisions.md` — strategic decisions log (D-numbered) constraining the copy/design work.

### `audio-summary/`
3 `.m4a` audio summaries (NotebookLM-style overviews of the research themes).

## Key Files
- `scrape_transcripts.py` — scraper that produced `transcripts/`. Hardcodes the episode slug list and the marketingpassivhaus.com page structure; re-run to refresh.

## Notes
- `episode-notes/` and `transcripts/` filenames are 1:1 — same slug in both.
- Synthesis dependency chain: transcripts → episode-notes → `marketing-rules.md` → alignment/redline/decisions.
- `homepage-copy-redline.md` STATUS and `positioning-decisions.md` are the live-status files — check them before assuming homepage copy state.
