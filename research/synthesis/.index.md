# synthesis — distilled marketing research → homepage decisions

The worked outputs of the homepage research (raw material in `../transcripts/`
and `../episode-notes/`). Chain: 38 episodes → `marketing-rules.md` → the
alignment audit, copy redline, and decisions log. Cross-referenced with
inline relative links. See `../.index.md`.

## Key Files
- `marketing-rules.md` — master rules distilled from all 38 episodes, each cited `[Guest — slug]`, with a Top-10 ranking and 8 thematic rule sections (Language, Audience, Format, Business Model, Proof, What Works/Fails, Client Psychology), plus tensions and quotes. Upstream source-of-truth for the other three docs.
- `marketing-rules.html` — rendered HTML copy of the above (regenerate from the `.md`; don't hand-edit).
- `homepage-alignment-and-business-model.md` — 7-part audit of `site/index.html` against the rules: exec summary, the "should we still say Passive House" tension, section-by-section scorecard, action list, copy rewrites, business-model critique, open decisions.
- `homepage-copy-redline.md` — section-by-section homepage edits keyed to `site/index.html` line ranges, each tagged KEEP/EDIT/ADD with P0 priority stars. Carries a STATUS block: no-data edits implemented in working tree, data-gated sections staged pending D4.
- `positioning-decisions.md` — D-numbered decisions log. D1 decided (B2B audience); D2/D3 open; **D4 open — the active blocker** (publishable proof).
