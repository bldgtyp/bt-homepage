## Summary
The trailer introduces the "Marketing Passivhaus" podcast, hosted by copywriter James Turner, whose stated mission is "spreading the word" about Passive House to people beyond those who already know it. Turner positions the show as the missing space focused purely on messaging and communication (not the technical building science), interviewing builders, architects, designers, suppliers, and owners about what's working and what's needed to grow the audience for high-performance building. Notable framing references include Bronwyn Barry's "boxy but beautiful" and Michael Eliason's defense of the "dumb box."

## Key Marketing Takeaways
- The core marketing job is "spreading the word about those benefits beyond those who already know" — i.e., reaching outside the existing PH-aware bubble.
- Lead with tangible owner benefits, not the standard: reliability, scalability, airtightness, minimal carbon/energy, improved air and health quality.
- A non-technical communicator (no architecture/engineering credential) can add value purely on messaging — selling PH is a distinct skill from building it.

## Language & Framing
- "boxy but beautiful" — Bronwyn Barry's simple, memorable call for building form.
- Reframe "dumb boxes" as a virtue: "the least expensive, the least carbon intensive, the most resilient, and have some of the lowest operational costs compared to a more varied and intensive massing" (Michael Eliason).
- Describe PH emotionally/sensorially: a "quiet, comfortable, low carbon fold" — comfort and health foregrounded over technical metrics.
- Benefit vocabulary used: reliability, scalability, airtightness, minimal carbon and energy use, improved air and health quality.

## Format & Channel
- Podcast as a word-spreading channel; affiliated/adjacent shows cited (Passive House Podcast, Passive House Accelerator).
- Substack as an audience-builder — Lloyd Alter's "Carbon Upfront" cited as the host's most-read newsletter.
- Building tours and conferences cited as "envy-inducing" content that draws people in.
- LinkedIn and email used as direct outreach/contact channels.
