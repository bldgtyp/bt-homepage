        <span class="section-label">WHY IT MATTERS</span>
        <h2 class="page-heading">Thermal bridges are both energy and durability problems.</h2>
        <p class="page-intro type-body-lg">
            A detail can look continuous in section and still create a meaningful psi-value, surface
